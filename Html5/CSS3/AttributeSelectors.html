Attribute Selectors

Thisi is a paragraph without the title attribute

p[title] selects all paragraphs containing the word, paragraph

p[title~="paragraph"] select all paragraphs with a title attribute containing the word, paragraph

p[title="#4 paragraph"] selects all paragraphs with a title attribute containing the exact text, #4 paragraph. Matches are case-sensetive and must match letter-for-letter including whitespace.
